⚠️ Snake Safety — Port Douglas Hiking
  • Wear closed shoes + long socks/pants — poisonous snakes are present and aggressive
  • Stick to well-marked trails only
  • Carry water, insect repellent, and sunscreen
  • Avoid hiking at dawn/dusk — peak snake activity times
  • Check trail conditions at visitor centres or local park websites before heading out

💡 Australia Travel Tips
  • Power adapters: Australia uses Type I plugs — bring an adapter
  • Currency: Australian Dollar (AUD). Most places accept cards; carry some cash for markets/small vendors
  • Tipping: Not expected in Australia, but appreciated for exceptional service
  • Sun protection: UV index is extreme — SPF 50+ sunscreen, hats, and rashguards essential
  • Reef-safe sunscreen: Required on the Great Barrier Reef
  • Water safety: Swim only between the flags at patrolled beaches
  • Emergency: 000 (Australian emergency number)
  • Time zones: Queensland (Cairns/Port Douglas) = UTC+10 · Sydney (AEST) = UTC+10 (no DST in April)
